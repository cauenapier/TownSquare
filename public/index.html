TownSquare

A tiny presence layer for websites

The web is full of content but empty of people. TownSquare gives your site a small shared place where visitors can see each other, walk around, rest by a tree, and say a few words — the feeling that someone else is here too, right now.

No accounts. Nothing stored. One snippet, and your page feels inhabited.
